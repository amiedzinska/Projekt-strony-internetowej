  1. Własności granic funkcji w punkcie
    2. Jeżeli istnieją granice: \( \displaystyle \lim_{x \to x_{0}} f(x)\), \( \displaystyle \lim_{x \to x_{0}} g(x)\) oraz \(c \in \mathbb{R}\), to:

    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to x_{0}} (c \cdot f(x)) = c \cdot \lim_{x \to x_{0}} f(x)\)
    • \( \displaystyle \lim_{x \to x_{0}} (f(x) + g(x)) = \lim_{x \to x_{0}} f(x) + \lim_{x \to x_{0}} g(x)\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to x_{0}} (f(x) - g(x)) = \lim_{x \to x_{0}} f(x) - \lim_{x \to x_{0}} g(x)\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to x_{0}} (f(x) \cdot g(x)) = \lim_{x \to x_{0}} f(x) \cdot \lim_{x \to x_{0}} g(x)\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to x_{0}} g(x) \ne 0,\) to: \(\displaystyle \lim_{x \to x_{0}} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \frac{\displaystyle\lim_{x \to x_{0}} f(x)}{\displaystyle\lim_{x \to x_{0}} g(x)}\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to x_{0}} g(x) = 0\) oraz \(g \ge 0\), to: \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to x_{0}} \sqrt{f(x)} = \sqrt{\displaystyle\lim_{x \to x_{0}} f(x)}\)

  2. Własności granic funkcji w nieskończoności
    3. Jeżeli istnieją granice: \( \displaystyle \lim_{x \to +\infty} f(x)\), \( \displaystyle \lim_{x \to +\infty} g(x)\) oraz \(c \in \mathbb{R}\), to:

    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to +\infty} (c \cdot f(x)) = c \cdot \lim_{x \to +\infty} f(x)\)
    • \( \displaystyle \lim_{x \to +\infty} (f(x) + g(x)) = \lim_{x \to +\infty} f(x) + \lim_{x \to +\infty} g(x)\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to +\infty} (f(x) - g(x)) = \lim_{x \to +\infty} f(x) - \lim_{x \to +\infty} g(x)\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to +\infty} (f(x) \cdot g(x)) = \lim_{x \to +\infty} f(x) \cdot \lim_{x \to +\infty} g(x)\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to +\infty} g(x) \ne 0,\) to: \(\displaystyle \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \frac{\displaystyle\lim_{x \to +\infty} f(x)}{\displaystyle\lim_{x \to +\infty} g(x)}\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to +\infty} g(x) = 0\) oraz \(g \ge 0\), to: \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to +\infty} \sqrt{f(x)} = \sqrt{\displaystyle\lim_{x \to +\infty} f(x)}\)
      • Powyższe własności są również prawdziwe w przypadku granicy funkcji w \(- \infty\).

  3. Ważne granice funkcji
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\tan x}{x} = 1\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\arcsin x}{x} = 1\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\arctan x}{x} = 1\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to \infty} \left(1+ \frac{a}{x}\right)^x = e^a\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\ln(1+x)}{x} = 1\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\log_{a}(1+x)}{x} = \log_{a}e \)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to \infty} \left(\frac{e^x -1}{x}\right)^x = 1\)
    • \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{a^x -1}{x} = \ln a\)
